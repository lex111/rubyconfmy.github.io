About Event

Our very own, very first, homegrown Ruby Conference.

RubyConf KL is a single day, weekend conference held by the Ruby Community in the Kuala Lumpur area. This event is targeted for the local tech community, and we welcome anyone from hardcode Ruby-ists to technology enthusiasts.

Expect awesome content, friendly community members and a wonderful time of networking.

So yeah ... it's like Ruby Tuesdays on steriods :D
