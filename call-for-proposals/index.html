Call for Proposals

We're looking for speakers to present in the following formats:

  • regular talks (30 minutes)
  • lightning talks (5 - 10 minutes)


If you need some inspirations, here are some suggested areas that you can talk about:

  • Ruby Core
  • Ruby Gems
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Day in the Life as a Ruby Programmer
  • Design Principles
  • Bleeding Edge
  • Community
  • DevOps
  • Front-end Development
  • Programming Languages
  • Mobile Development
  • Open Source
  • Project Management
  • Security
  • Stories of Battles Lost & Won


Please send in your proposals by 15th July 2017, 23:59:59 UTC.
